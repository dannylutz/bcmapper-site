Common questions

FAQ

Is bcMAPPER a BCR2000 editor for Mac as well?

Yes. bcMAPPER is designed to be a modern editor for BCR2000 presets and banks on macOS and Windows. A Linux build is coming very soon too!

Does it also support the BCF2000?

Yes — bcMAPPER supports both the Behringer BCR2000 and BCF2000.

Can I import and export SysEx?

Yes. bcMAPPER supports importing and exporting SysEx for presets and full banks (32 presets).

Can I print scribble strips?

Yes. You can generate printable scribble strips sized for the hardware and include color sections.

Do you use heavy DRM?

No. The licensing goal is "polite deterrent", and we've priced it to be fairly accessible. However, if you are in a position that you can't afford to support the project, or are in a position to buy an extra copy for someone who can't, please let us know and we will match that and give two copies to those who are unable to afford it.
